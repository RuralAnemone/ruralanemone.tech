Bookmarklets

HOW TO:

Drag the links to your bookmark/favorites bar to turn them into bookmarklets.

To use your bookmarklet, just click it while a page is open.

To preview what the bookmarklet does, click on the link

Note: Bookmarklets usually do not work in Chrome New Tab / Chrome configuration pages
